Accompanying

I have a lot of experience providing piano accompaniment for exams, workshops, rehearsals and concerts and enjoy the collaborative element of this work. I have accompanied many ABRSM and Trinity instrumental exams and diplomas as well as university recitals. Whilst at university I accompanied Opera Society rehearsals and played for the Bristol University Choral Society rehearsals. For 4 years I played for the Bristol Hippodrome community choir in workshops, rehearsals and performances.

Prices

My accompanying rate differs depending on the nature and regularity of the commitment and the complexity of the music required. For music exam accompaniment I charge between £25 and £40 for the exam day depending on the length and difficulty of the examination. There would be an additional charge for rehearsal time and a travel fee may be included depending on location.

Please contact me for more details, availability and to discuss pricing options.
