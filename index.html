Piano and music theory tuition in Pembroke.

I'm a self-employed piano teacher, music theory teacher and accompanist in Pembroke with over 12 years of professional experience. I've taught students from the ages of 4 to 55 ranging from absolute beginner to advanced (beyond grade 8) and welcome these opportunities to meet a wide range of interesting people.

Through playing the piano I’ve learnt to challenge and express myself and practise discipline and organisation. For me, music is about expression, growth, history and creativity. I’d love to share that with you.

Please explore my website for information about my experience, teaching style and more.

If you're interested in learning to play piano, learning music theory or in need of an accompanist, please get in touch by ringing 07443512806 or by sending me an email at on1001@my.bristol.ac.uk.
