    • Piano Lessons

    I strongly believe in student-led learning, so tailor my lessons to each individual depending on what their needs and aspirations are. I take some of my students through the ABRSM or Trinity practical exams, or just work on repertoire including classical pieces, popular songs or Disney tunes! I have also worked on notation with students who are keen to write down their own compositions. I will always find or make the resources necessary for the best tuition of each student.

    I travel to most of my students by bike after school hours, or see them in my home during the day. Most of my lessons are 30 mins long, although for young beginners I suggest 20 mins, and some prefer to have up to 45 mins or an hour. All lesson prices are calculated from my hourly rate of £33 per hour.

    I organise 2 concerts a year, every Christmas and summer, for which I encourage all of my students to prepare a piece to perform. All students families and friends are invited and we spend a fun and informal afternoon together, hearing everyone’s performances and enjoying some food and drinks afterwards!
