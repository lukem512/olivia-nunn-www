Lessons

Teaching Style

I have a firm belief in student-led learning and, therefore, tailor my lessons to each individual according to their needs and aspirations. Many of my students chose to progress through the ABRSM or Trinity exams (the two main exam boards for instrumental examinations), but others prefer to work on their own repertoire choices which may include classical pieces, pop songs, film scores and Disney tunes!

Some students are keen composers and we can work on notation skills allowing them to write down their own work. I also incorporate music theory with practical piano skills encouraging a deeper, more logical understanding of the music and giving students knowledge which can be transferred to another instrument or musical practice. I’m very happy guiding you down whichever path you chose and helping you decide if you’re not sure. I will always do everything in my power to find or make the resources necessary for the best tuition of each student.

Music Theory

Separate music theory tuition is also available and I can help students prepare for music theory examinations. Often this is most applicable to students working towards higher level ABRSM instrumental or singing exams as ABRSM requires a pass at grade 5 music theory before being able to take any exam beyond grade 5, but it is an option open to anyone.

Group music theory sessions are also available given enough interest. Please get in touch to discuss details regarding this.

Timings

I usually teach throughout the school terms and often also during the holidays, depending on availability. I try to keep my teaching Monday - Thursday but depending on individual situations other arrangements may be possible.

I love to teach regular, weekly lessons as it encourages a strong bond between teacher and student and allows the student to learn and improve more steadily (with self-led practice at home, of course!). However, I understand this is not an option available to everybody and am very happy to teach one off or ad hoc lessons as well.

Location

I am available for lessons at my home in Narberth. If you’d like to learn but aren’t able to come to me I can travel, so please still get in touch. Travelling to a lesson may incur an additional charge depending on how far I’d need to travel.

Student Concerts

Often, at the end of the summer and winter terms, I will organise a piano concert for my students. Taking part is not compulsory but I encourage all of my students to prepare a piece to perform. Families and friends are invited, some also perform and concerts are by no means limited to piano! We spend a fun and informal afternoon hearing everyone’s performances and enjoying some food and drinks together afterwards!
