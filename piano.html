    • Piano Lessons

    All lessons are calculated from my hourly rate, £33.

    20 mins (£11) recommended for young beginners (aged 4-7).

    30 mins (£16.50) for students aged 8 or over, students who have had over a year of lessons, or anyone working towards grade 1-4.

    45 mins (£24.75) for students working towards grade 5-7.

    60 mins (£33) for students working towards grade 8.

    I strongly believe in student-led learning, so tailor my lessons to each individual depending on what their needs and aspirations are. I take some of my students through the ABRSM or Trinity exams, or just work on repertoire including classical pieces, popular songs or Disney tunes! I have also worked on notation with students who are keen to write down their own compositions. I always find or make the resources necessary for the best tuition of each student.

    I travel by bicycle to most of my students houses after school hours, or teach some adult students in my home during the day.

    Music theory tuition is also available. I incorporate some theory into piano lessons anyway as I believe it significantly aids understanding of piano technique and reading music. I can also prepare students wanting to take a music theory exam. Often this is most applicable to students working towards an ABRSM instrumental exam (piano or any other instrument, including singing) grade 6 or higher, as this is an ABRSM requirement

    Mostly I teach during the school term times but can sometimes be available for lessons during the holidays as well.

    At the end of each Christmas and summer term I organise a piano concert, for which I encourage all of my students to prepare a piece to perform. Students families and friends are invited and we spend a fun and informal aftertoon hearing everyone’s performances and enjoying some food and drinks together afterwards!
