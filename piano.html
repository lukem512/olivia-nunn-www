Piano Lessons

All lessons are calculated using an hourly rate of £34. This figure is recommended by the Musicians' Union. See below for a complete price list.

Teaching Style

I strongly believe in student-led learning and tailor my lessons to each individual, depending on their needs and aspirations. I take many of my students through the ABRSM or Trinity exams, but some just work on their repertoire including classical pieces, popular songs and even Disney tunes! I have also worked on notation with students who are keen to write down their own compositions. I always find or make the resources necessary for the best tuition of each student.

Seperate music theory tuition is also available. I incorporate some theory into piano lessons anyway as I believe it significantly aids understanding of piano technique and reading music. I can also prepare students wanting to take a music theory exam. Often this is most applicable to students working towards an ABRSM instrumental exam (piano or any other instrument, including singing) grade 6 or higher, as this is an ABRSM requirement.

I am available for lessons within term times and often outside of these. Please contact me for more information.

Location

I am available for lessons in Bristol and in Cambridge. I usually teach at my students' houses although I can also teach at my home if that is preferred or a piano required. I usually travel by bicycle to my students' houses.

Student Concerts

I often organise a piano concert at the end of the Summer and Winter terms, for which I encourage all of my students to prepare a piece to perform. Students families and friends are invited and we spend a fun and informal aftertoon hearing everyone’s performances and enjoying some food and drinks together afterwards!
